The University of Cambridge

The University of Cambridge is the second-oldest university in the English-speaking world. The University at present has more than 18,000 full-time students - over 12,000 undergraduates and over 6,000 postgraduates. The University also has a worldwide reputation for other aspects of its work. Cambridge University Press (one of the world's oldest and largest publishers) and UCLES (University of Cambridge Local Examinations Syndicate) are world leaders in their respective fields and allow the University to make a direct educational and academic contribution to the lives of millions of people around the world.
