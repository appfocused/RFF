The Aubin Cinema

  • 64-66 Redchurch Street,
    London, E2 7DP

The cinema and bar at The Aubin are run by Shoreditch House, part of the Soho House group. The Aubin & Wills designed interior provides comfort and style for up to 45 cinema goers, who can recline in the velvet armchairs and sofas whilst enjoying a selection of drinks and snacks from the fully licensed bar.
